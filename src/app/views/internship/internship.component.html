Internship Program

Alpha Chaperone promises a unique Internship Experience to the clients by providing tailored tasks and systematic monitoring policies in collaboration with renowned Socially responsible organisations engaged in various socially responsible domains and we allocate internship slots with them.

Role of Alpha Chaperone

As the name suggests, we play the role of a chaperone; we look after the interns by providing proper guidelines and quality assured monitoring methods. We have our unique Mobile App: Alpha Chaperone for managing and monitoring the whole internship programme. This is the first of its kind and exclusively designed for social workers. Our company will formulate semester and specialisation specific daily tasks and objectives in accordance with the projects available at the organisation. This helps both the organisation as well as interns to achieve their objectives and requirements. The attendance, daily work progress, task completion and other affairs will be managed and supervised by Alpha Chaperone. And by successful completion of the internship programme, will be awarded with a detailed E-Certificate.
