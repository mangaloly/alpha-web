Who we are?

Alpha Chaperone is a group of highly motivated Social Workers, who aims to redefine the standards of the profession through monitored internship programmes and quality training sessions.

As the name suggests, We identify ourselves as Chaperones; a person who accompanies and looks after another person or group of people. And we are prime of our kind in the domain of social work. Hence we chose the name ‘Alpha Chaperone’. We focus on providing tailored internship experiences with specifically designed tasks and monitoring patterns and equip social workers with a set of professional skills and attitudes through training and workshops to mould themself as better professionals and persons which will help them to contribute to every area they touch.

Our Vision

“To enhance the quality of professional Social Work practises, by moulding the budding professionals.”

Our Mission
  • To ensure the quality of internships through specifically designed tasks and monitoring patterns, that helps to enhance the productivity and learnings of the Social Work trainees.
  • To deliver standard Training sessions with relevant and updated content, to equip the social work trainees with professional skill sets and mindset.
