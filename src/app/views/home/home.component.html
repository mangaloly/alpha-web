Who We Are

We are a group of highly
motivated Social Workers!

We aim to redefine the standards of the profession through monitored internship programmes and quality assured training sessions.

As the name suggests, We identify ourselves as Chaperones; a person who accompanies and looks after another person or group of people. And we are prime of our kind in the domain of social work. Hence we chose the name ‘Alpha Chaperone’.

  • Quality assured training & Services
  • Expert team members
  • State of the art Internship monitoring
